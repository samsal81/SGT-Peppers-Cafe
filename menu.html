The Menu

Sgt. Peppers has a full menu serving breakfast and lunch at any time of the day all veggies are prepped to day, and cook to order in all our omelettes and skillets for a fresh flavor. Our chefs prepare two types of homemade cheese sauces a spicy white and yellow cheddar cheese, sausage gravy, beef & bean chili, sweet pancake and waffle batter, most of our salads are served w/ freshly cut romaine lettuce.
