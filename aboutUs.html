About Us

Sgt. Pepper’s Cafe is a family owned business which first open in 1999. Outstanding young hard working crew. Trying to build something that can create a good living for all the workers at this establishment besides just the owner. To show our appreciation to the community and surrounding areas on Christmas and thanksgiving we serve a homemade Christmas and thanksgiving meals on those holidays for those in need and a place to go to enjoy the holiday. If it wasn't for our solid customer base, we wouldn't be able to give back nor give opportunity for our workers to make a better living while enjoying there work and time here.
